Cranberry Meatballs

These cranberry meatballs are great and very simple to make if you are looking for the perfect sweet-and-savory appetizer for Thanksgiving or Christmas.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • ⅓ cup ketchup
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 (16 ounce) can jellied cranberry sauce
  • 1 (18 ounce) bottle BBQ sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar, or to taste
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Mix ground beef, bread crumbs, ketchup, eggs, soy sauce, black pepper, and garlic powder in a large bowl until well combined. Shape into small meatballs and arrange on a nonstick baking sheet.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until browned, about 30 minutes, turning once.
  4. Meanwhile, combine cranberry sauce, BBQ sauce, brown sugar, and lemon juice in a large saucepan; heat and stir over low heat until smooth. Add meatballs and simmer for I hour. Serve warm.
